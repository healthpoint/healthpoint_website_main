About Us

HealthPoint Pediatrics was opened in the fall of 2004 with a goal of bringing personalized, expert pediatric care to Fayette and surrounding counties. We base everything we do on trust and mutual respect.

We offer preventive care for healthy children and treatment for those who are acutely or chronically ill and try to blend together both traditional and alternative views on medicine. We use this integration to develop unique and individualized approaches to the diagnosis and management of many medical and behavioral problems, such as chronic abdominal pain, asthma, allergies, ADHD, autism, and many others.
